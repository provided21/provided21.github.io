I'm **Haoran Hu** (胡浩然), a sophomore undergraduate in the **Big Data Communication Experimental Class** at the **School of Journalism and Communication, Nanjing University** (南京大学). I work at the intersection of computational methods and communication studies, with a focus on large language models and their real-world applications.

**GPA: 4.4 / 5.0** (Rank 2 in major)

## Research Interests

- **Model Routing & LLM Applications** — building efficient and adaptive routing mechanisms for large language models
- **Computational Social Science** — using computational methods to study communication and social phenomena
- **Data-Driven Communication** — applying data science and machine learning to communication research

## Current Work

I'm a member of the **[LAMDA Lab](https://www.lamda.nju.edu.cn/)** (led by Prof. Lijun Zhang), where I contribute to **RoutingToolBox** — a benchmark pipeline for LLM routing evaluation. My work involves:

- Processing multiple datasets (Mixinstruct, Routereval, MMRbench) for benchmark construction
- Developing algorithm interfaces and utility functions
- Independently building the **RouteJudge** application (full-stack)

I have three second-author papers on LLM routing:

- **"RouterJudge: Preference-Based Evaluation of LLM Routers under Pluralistic User Preferences"** — accepted at **ICML Workshop** 🎉
- **"Priors in LLM Routing: From Initialization to Regularization"** — under review at **TMLR**
- **"From Sampled Outcomes to Capability Distributions: Rethinking Supervision for LLM Routing"** — under review at **EMNLP**
