This knowledge graph project maps the landscape of digital marketing communication through 14 chapter notes, 19 concept deep-dives, and 8 cross-cutting mechanism analyses. Built in Obsidian with Mermaid.js diagrams and AI-assisted discussion, it demonstrates a student-centered approach to knowledge synthesis.

**Key Features:**
- Three-layer knowledge architecture (chapters → concepts → mechanisms)
- 41 SVG structure diagrams and 41 Mermaid relationship maps
- Human-AI collaborative note-taking with transparent attribution
- Graph-based visualization of conceptual connections

**Tools:** Obsidian, Mermaid.js, Python, Claude
