  <p class="home-kicker">Current Focus</p>
  <h2>Model routing, evaluation, and useful LLM systems.</h2>
  <p>I contribute to <strong>RoutingToolBox</strong>, a benchmark pipeline for evaluating LLM routing. My work includes dataset processing, algorithm interfaces, utility functions, and the independent full-stack development of <strong>RouteJudge</strong>, an application for preference-based routing evaluation.</p>
